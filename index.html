Grigore Vieru

"After grass, the most trampled is modesty."

Here is some information about Grigore Vieru's life:

Grigore Vieru was a Moldavian poet, writer and unionist advocate. Known for his poems and books for children. His poetry is characterized by vivid natural scenery, patriotism, as well as a venerated image of the sacred mother.

In 1988 he was awarded the most prestigious international distinction for children's book - Andersen's Award. He was elected Honorary Member of the Romanian Academy and in 1992 and was also recommended for the Nobel Peace Prize.
